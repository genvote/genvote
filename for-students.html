For Students

Our GenVote members and teams are the center of our network. Our students are at the forefront of local student opinions research on their college campuses and are elevating our generation's voice through innovative youth platforms.

From conducting research to developing policies and partnering with candidates, GenVote members are changing the way that we think about politic engagement on college campuses. Interested in bringing a Generation Vote program to your campus? Tell us more about yourself and join us today!
