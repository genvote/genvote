OUR GENERATION IS FACING A

DEMOCRATIC CRISIS

The past two elections proved that our generation is a force to be reckoned with. Half of our generation, ages 18-29, voted in the last major presidential election and broke records across the country.


But since 2020, politicians across the country are aggressively moving legislation to suppress the vote and spreading the “Big Lie” of a stolen election in our homes, schools and neighborhoods. One year ago, on January 6th, 2021, the “Big Lie” got so bad that it literally led to a violent attack on the U.S. Capitol in Washington D.C.-– the first attack since the War of 1812.


The January 6th insurrection and the recent failure of the U.S. Senate to pass the Freedom to Vote John Lewis Act exposed how fragile our democracy is. And in a series of devastating blows to the Voting Rights Act, even the Supreme Court of the United States is unwilling to prevent our democracy from reaching a breaking point.


This open warfare against our democracy is fueled by an effort to silence the most racially and ethnically diverse generation that our country has ever seen. And they are waging this war because they are afraid of the power that YOU hold. From New Hampshire, to Texas and Wisconsin, we’ve seen young people forced to jump over countless hurdles just to exercise our rights.


Our politicians and courts have failed us. But unlike them, we have no choice but to continue fighting. The forces behind anti-voter laws that suppress the youth vote, gerrymandered maps and outright election sabotage will only be emboldened by the Senate’s failure to act and the Supreme Court’s unwillingness to safeguard voting rights at the federal level.


The only thing that can overcome the attacks on our democracy is our generation rising to the moment and reimagining what a just democracy for all looks like in this country. Generation Vote is ready to do just that and we need your help to spark this movement.

WHAT WE DO

Generation Vote sparks an intersectional, youth-led movement that fights for our right to vote and a just democracy for all.


WHO WE ARE


Welcome to Generation Vote.

We are a cross-class, multiracial movement of young people tearing down the barriers that prevent young people from participating in our democracy. GenVoters across the country are rising up to reclaim our democracy and make electoral justice a major priority for our generation. Are you ready to join us?
